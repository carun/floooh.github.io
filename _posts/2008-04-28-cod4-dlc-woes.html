--- layout: post title: COD4 DLC woes... date: '2008-04-28T10:43:00.005+01:00' author: Andre Weissflog tags: - Gaming modified_time: '2008-04-28T11:08:25.358+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2948438400037317662.post-5020143699289829549 blogger_orig_url: http://flohofwoe.blogspot.com/2008/04/cod4-dlc-woes.html --- Here's an interesting side-effect of the COD4 map pack: ever since I downloaded the map pack my K/D-ratio started to suffer terribly. Once I deleted the map pack from my HD the ratio immediately improved. Looks like the map-pack was mainly purchased by hardcore players, so that there are not enough noob players like me available for fair match-making. So the DLC actually made the game less enjoyable for me. Well, I didn't like Chinatown anyway ;)
