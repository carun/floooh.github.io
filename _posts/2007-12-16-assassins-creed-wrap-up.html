--- layout: post title: Assassin's Creed Wrap Up date: '2007-12-16T23:09:00.000+01:00' author: Andre Weissflog tags: - Gaming modified_time: '2007-12-16T23:58:25.679+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2948438400037317662.post-3355049516463960868 blogger_orig_url: http://flohofwoe.blogspot.com/2007/12/assassins-creed-wrap-up.html --- I finally reserved a few hours to finish up on Assassin's Creed. Yawn... all this cheap pseudo-science crap was really getting on my nerves towards the end. It seems that every stupid conspiracy theory ever invented has been stuffed into AC's "story".

The game itself was actually quite a chore during the last few hours, because nothing really unexpected or new happened (yes, the game tries to have some sort of a surprise ending, but one can see this coming from a mile away). For the little variety the game-play offers, the game just feels too long IMHO. Everything is just too stretched-out. Overall it's a pretty good game, but now that it's over I feel somewhat ... unsatisfied. The fact that the game doesn't seem to have a real ending doesn't help either. Yes, I discovered the credits ... but what the hell now?
